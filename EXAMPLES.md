# Example tactical questions

## Smart Context

- What are the immediate tactical implications of the observed image stream?
- What are the recommended prioritisation of actions?
- Provide the SITREP on observed images and messages.
- Is there any coordinated movement across all sectors?

## Video context

- How many military vehicles are observed in the image stream?
- Make a best guest estimate of the strength of the observed forces?
- What is the likely intent of the observed forces?
